Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is a boisterous and energetic ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village. Orphaned at a young age, Naruto yearns for recognition and dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village. Driven by a strong will and unwavering determination, he faces numerous challenges and overcomes them with his signature jutsu, the Rasengan.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha, a prodigy from a prestigious clan, is a stoic and introspective ninja consumed by a thirst for revenge. After witnessing the tragic destruction of his clan, Sasuke embarks on a dark path, seeking power to avenge his family. Despite his cold demeanor, Sasuke possesses immense talent and a deep-seated desire for strength. I hope this brief overview is helpful!
